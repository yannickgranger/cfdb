- Do NOT add a UDF registration. RFC §6 non-goals.
- Do NOT extend the Cypher parser. RFC §2.2 — `cfdb-query` is UNCHANGED by this slice.
- Do NOT introduce a `.param-resolver-baseline.toml` or allowlist file. §6.8 no-metric-ratchets.
- Do NOT change any `pub` API on existing crates. `cfdb_concepts::load_concept_overrides` is CONSUMED as-is; its signature stays frozen.
- Do NOT perform boy-scout refactors inside `cfdb-cli/src/` on unrelated files (drift god-files tracked separately in #151).

  ```rust

      db: &std::path::Path,
      keyspace: &str,
      workspace_root: &std::path::Path,
      name: &str,
      cli_params: &[String],
  ```

- **Pipeline (locked by discovery Chain C):**
  1. `let path = workspace_root.join(".cfdb/predicates").join(format!("{name}.cypher"));`
  2. `let cypher = std::fs::read_to_string(&path).map_err(|e| format!("read predicate {}: {e}", path.display()))?;`
